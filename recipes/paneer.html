Paneer Butter Masala

Description

Paneer Butter Masala, also known as butter paneer is a rich & creamy curry made with paneer, spices, onions, tomatoes, cashews and butter. As the name denotes, the curry is cooked in butter which imparts it's characteristic buttery flavor to the dish. It is one of the most popular dishes in Indian restaurants similar to kadai paneer, Palak Paneer, Matar Paneer and Malai Kofta.
